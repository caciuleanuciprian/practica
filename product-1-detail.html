20/21 La Liga Home Match Shirt - MESSI

$50.00

Product Details

Match shirt from the 20/21 FC Barcelona Home Kit. The new 20/21 kit reclaims the traditional vertical blue-and-scarlet stripes in its design, and newly incorporates a fine yellow line between them. This new pattern draws inspiration from the century-old Club crest worn in 1920, which incorporated a yellow line alongside each blue-and-scarlet stripe. The senyera flag on the back of the shirt, just below the collar, reinforces the connection between the team and Catalan spirit.
